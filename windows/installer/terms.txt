LOCKROOT TERMS AND CONDITIONS

Last updated: 2026-05-20

Lockroot is a local password manager. It stores encrypted vault data on the user's device and does not provide an account, sync service, cloud recovery service, or password recovery backdoor.

By installing or using Lockroot, you agree to the following:

1. No recovery

Lockroot cannot recover a lost master password. If the master password is forgotten, the encrypted vault cannot be decrypted.

2. Local responsibility

You are responsible for protecting your device, choosing a strong master password, and keeping encrypted exports or backups in a safe place.

3. Encrypted export/import

Export files are encrypted with a separate export password. Import requires the same export password. Lockroot cannot recover that password.

4. Security limits

Lockroot protects vault data at rest. It cannot fully protect secrets from a compromised operating system, malicious keyboard, malicious accessibility tooling, screen recording by another device, or physical observation.

Lockroot is provided as-is, without warranty of any kind. Use it at your own risk.
